Hebei's ex-senior political adviser expelled from Party
          
           
  
 
   A file photo of Ai Wenli 
  
 
Ai Wenli, former senior political adviser in North China's Hebei province, has been expelled from the Party for multiple offenses and his assets illegally acquired will be confiscated. 
His case will be transferred to the prosecuting department, and a lenient punishment will be suggested.
