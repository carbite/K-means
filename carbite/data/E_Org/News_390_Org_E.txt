Chinese vice premier to attend WEF on ASEAN in Hanoi
          
          BEIJING -- Chinese Vice Premier Hu Chunhua will attend the World Economic Forum (WEF) on ASEAN 2018 in Hanoi, Vietnam from September 11 to 13, Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Hua Chunying announced on Friday.
Hu's visit is at the invitation of Vietnamese Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc.
