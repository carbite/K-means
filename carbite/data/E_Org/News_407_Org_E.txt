China adopts new personal income tax law
          
           
  
 
   [Photo/VCG] 
  
 
China's top legislature passed the amendment to the personal income tax law on Friday, raising&nbsp;minimum threshold for individual income tax from 3,500 to 5,000 yuan per month, or 60,000 yuan per year. 
The new threshold would come into effect on Oct 1.
