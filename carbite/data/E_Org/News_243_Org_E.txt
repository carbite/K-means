Two former senior officials held on corruption charges
          
           
  
 
   Wang Xiaoguang (left) and Zhang Shaochun. [China Daily] 
  
 
The Supreme People's Procuratorate has ordered arrest of Wang Xiaoguang, former vice-governor of Guizhou province, and Zhang Shaochun, former vice-minister of finance, on suspicion of corruption and bribery.
