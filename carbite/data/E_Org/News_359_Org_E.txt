Key documents of Party congress published in 7 languages
          
          BEIJING -- A book of 11 important documents of the 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China (CPC) has been published in seven foreign languages, according to an announcement on Tuesday.
The book, published by the Foreign Languages Press, is now available in English, French, Spanish, German, Japanese, Russian and Arabic.
The documents include the main report at the National Congress and the communique of the first plenary session of the 19th CPC Central Committee.
The 19th National Congress of the CPC was held in Beijing last October.
