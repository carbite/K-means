President of All-China Women's Federation elected
          
           
  
 
   Shen Yueyue (3rd R) is re-elected president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF) at the first plenary meeting of the 12th executive committee of the ACWF in Beijing, capital of China, on Nov. 1, 2018. The meeting also elected 14 people as the federation's vice presidents and 40 as its standing committee members. [Photo/Xinhua] 
  
 
Shen Yueyue was re-elected president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF) on Thursday by a vote. 
The first plenary meeting of the 12th executive committee of the ACWF also elected 14 people as the federation's vice presidents and 40 as its standing committee members.
